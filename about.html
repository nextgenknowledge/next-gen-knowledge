Started by students who saw a gap in practical education.

Next-Gen Knowledge was founded by four high school students on a simple, powerful realization: the most important skills for the future—financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and critical thinking—are rarely taught in a way that resonates with middle school students.

We started by teaching a pilot course, Money Masters, to bridge the gap between abstract academic concepts and real-world application. What began as an experiment has grown into a mission-driven organization dedicated to equipping students in grades 5–8 with the tools they need to navigate the modern economy.

The Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to practical, high-value education. By utilizing a student-led mentorship model, we remove the intimidation of traditional classroom settings and replace it with peer-to-peer engagement. We believe that when students are empowered to teach and learn from one another, they develop more than just knowledge—they develop the confidence to lead.

Our Core Values

  • Practical over theoretical
  • Student-led empowerment
  • Accessibility for every family
  • Innovation in learning
